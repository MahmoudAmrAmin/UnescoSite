UNESCO Future Builders Club

The initiative is a key element in Egypt's digital transformation strategy, investing in students' digital skills through specialized training programs and workshops in various technological fields to enable Egypt to build a more prosperous future.

The scholarship is targeted at all students and graduates from Egyptian universities and higher institutes in the Arab Republic of Egypt.

The initiative aims to prepare a generation of specialists in the field of information technology capable of competing effectively and actively participating in building Digital Egypt.

Vision

Achieving leadership in the field of communications and information technology by building a skilled and innovative workforce that leads the comprehensive digital transformation locally and regionally.
